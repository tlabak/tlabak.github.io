Overview

The embarkment of an adventurous journey at RATA, encompassing the entire startup spectrum, from the oversight of organizational structure, business and financial modeling, graphic design, pitching, IP, advertising, and market research, to the presentation of funding proposals. RATA, the startup company, holds the philosophy of merging arts and engineering to specialize in creative solutions. One product developed is a musical instrument that enables pressureless interaction for musicians, particularly those with muscular impairments in the hands or wrists. This innovative device allows music to be played without applying pressure, promoting a comfortable and enjoyable experience. The instrument finds applications in Physical Therapy/Occupational Hand Therapy, seamlessly integrating musical elements into therapeutic exercises. Its aspiration is to provide universal access to musical expression. Further developments on this venture are currently at a halt.
